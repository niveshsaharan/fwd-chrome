Service Notes

Checked services are eligible for rate-shopping or matching store overrides. Unchecked services are skipped until they are turned back on.

  • Michaels: UPS® Ground (UPS) - Michaels controls only Michaels orders requesting ups ground; regular UPS® Ground (UPS) remains separate.
  • Store overrides: Michaels ups ground and ups ground saver apply directly when their matching service toggle is enabled. If disabled, normal rate-shopping is used.
  • Skip already-correct: when enabled, matching selections are left alone if service, package, dimensions, and bill-to account are already correct.
  • Amazon Shipping: used only for domestic non-expedited requests and skipped for Walmart store orders.
  • USPS Priority Mail: requested USPS Priority Mail orders are narrowed to USPS Priority Mail candidates.
